Our Chotu Sa Story

With the intention of staying together as a family, the elder son, Arpit Kapoor, moved Kapoor Aunty & Uncle from Delhi to Bangalore in June 2014 and opened Kapoor's Café. Since then, it’s been a journey of pure love, pure veg, and pure Punjabi spices!

2014 • The Seed is Sown

Started as a small 36-seater restaurant in Bangalore to keep Mom & Dad busy and happy.

2015 • The Doctor Joins

The younger son, Dr. Apoorv Kapoor, joined the family business, choosing spices over stethoscopes!

2018 • Spreading the Love

Opened our first franchise with a dear friend. Since then, friends have built the brand together.

2021 • Delhi Wali Chaats

Launched Kapoor's Chaat Bhandaar to satisfy Bangalore's craving for authentic Purani Dilli street-food.

2023 • South Meets North

Launched Shree KC Mess serving authentic tastes of Andhra after 9 months of rigorous regional research.

NOW • 15 Branches & Growing

Delighting 3 cities—Bangalore, Mysore & Hyderabad. Plus, introducing Kapoor's Resorts & Events at Varthur with a 500+ capacity AC hall!
